What is Jossware?

Jossware is a technology consulting firm that has been helping clients solve technical problems since 2008. If you have vision, drive, and a singular focus on achieving business success, I'd love to be a part of your team. I strive to build partnerships through open communication and technical leadership, with the ultimate goal of delivering real business value. Whether your business is looking to increase revenue, streamline inefficient operations, reduce risk or cut overhead, I can apply technology to help you achieve the desired end result.

How Can Jossware Help?

Jossware can help businesses at all stages. I have worked with large, established companies producing millions of dollars of revenue to small, self-funded solo-founder startups. The majority of my clients are early stage bootstrapped startups, often with a single, non-technical founder. If you have an idea for a technology driven business or software application, but lack the technical know-how to move your raw idea from concept to reality, Jossware can make it happen.

Jossware specializes in full-stack web application development. I can build software applications from the ground up, or work to enhance pre-existing applications. Internal tools, customer-facing applications, e-commerce or marketing sites - I have the broad expertise and experience to get these done.

Mamajamas

Mamajamas gives you a personalized list of baby gear to customize and share with other parents.

Working alongside a talented designer, we built Mamajamas as a custom Rails application. Technologies like postgresql, backbone.js, memcache, redis and sidekiq allowed us to build a robust and responsive application that customers enjoy using. Mamajamas also integrates with several third-party services such as Amazon Web Services and Mailchimp. Product updates can be deployed quickly and confidently thanks to a thorough test suite and continuous integration server.
